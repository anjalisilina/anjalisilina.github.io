About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I'm a Computer Science and Finance major at Northeastern University, passionate about the intersection of AI and finance. I've worked as a software developer at Code4Community, where I built features used by real nonprofits, and as a cybersecurity intern at NETSCOUT, where I worked on network threat detection tools and an auditing project.

I'm also the co-organizer of a hackathon through the Shooting Stars Foundation, where I help create spaces for students to build impactful tech projects. I'm currently a fellow in the Girls Into VC program, deepening my knowledge of venture capital and startup ecosystems — especially in fintech, AI, and healthtech.

My biggest technical project right now is FinAgent, a multi-agent RAG system that uses Azure AI Search and OpenAI LLMs to analyze earnings reports and transcripts. It's my way of combining everything I love — AI, finance, and building real tools that solve real problems. Outside of class and work, I enjoy exploring new technologies, reading about early-stage startups, and thinking about how AI will reshape financial infrastructure. I'm always looking for opportunities to collaborate, build, and learn. Feel free to explore my site and reach out!
